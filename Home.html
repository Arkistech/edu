About us

Arkis Tech supports students and teachers with any hardware or software issues. Our goal is to extend the lifespan of devices by providing expert troubleshooting, repair, and maintenance guidance, which promotes sustainable practices. By keeping devices in good condition and improving their performance, we reduce the need for unnecessary replacements, minimizing electronic waste. We strive to build confidence in using technology and encourage everyone to care for their devices, ensuring long-term use.
